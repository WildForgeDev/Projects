~A little about us~

• Django Pizza was founded in 1992 by four college friends in Colorado Springs, Colorado. As college students one of their staple diets was pizza and they were not satisfied with the options available to them at the time. What started out as a mere frustration morphed into an inspiration to throw the pizza market on its head. Chris, Tyler, Jeffery, and Richard pooled their meager resources together and opened their first brick oven pizza restaurant in April 1992. Since then, they have expanded to 3 separate locations throughout the greater Colorado Springs and have been named the best pizza restaurant ongoing since 2016. Django Brick Oven Pizza continues to strive to provide the best pizza with the freshest ingredients coupled with outstanding customer service and hopes to expand their legacy to the Denver metro area by 2022.

• Django’s notoriety is their brick oven pizzas. However, Django’s also offers sides such as breadsticks, cheesy bread, various flavors of chicken wings as well as select beverages. While delivery is limited to pizza, sides and non-alcoholic beverages, each location also offers a variety of local craft beer on tap to help support our local microbreweries.
